About Talha Labels

Woven with Precision, Trusted Across Borders

Talha Labels is a custom woven label manufacturer based in Sialkot, Pakistan — a city long known for its craftsmanship in textiles and sporting goods. Every label that leaves our facility carries that same standard of precision.

We specialize in premium woven labels for garment brands, factories, and private-label businesses, with a dedicated focus on serving clients across the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, and Oman. Reliability, accuracy, and customer satisfaction guide every order we produce.
